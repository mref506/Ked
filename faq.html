Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kredoswift affiliated with Safaricom or any other mobile provider?

No. Kredoswift operates independently and is not affiliated with Safaricom, Airtel, or Telkom.

How fast is the airtime to cash conversion process?

Transactions are processed almost typically within 1 - 30 minutes.

Can I receive money on a different number from the one I used to send airtime?

Payouts are only made to the same number used during the airtime transfer.

What are the limits for converting airtime to M-Pesa?

You can convert amounts ranging from Ksh 50 to Ksh 5,000 per transaction. For larger sums, multiple transactions are advised.

Do you charge extra fees for M-Pesa withdrawals?

No, all transactions through Kredoswift are free of M-Pesa transaction fees.

Do I get a discount when buying airtime from Kredoswift?

Yes! Enjoy discounts of up to 10% when purchasing airtime through our platform.

Does the discount apply to Airtel and Telkom airtime?

Currently, discounts apply to Safaricom airtime. Support for Airtel and Telkom is coming soon.

Can I make a purchase if I have an active Okoa Jahazi or Fuliza balance?

Yes. Transactions are possible even with an active Okoa or Fuliza balance, provided you haven't hit your limits.

What types of airtime are accepted for conversion?

We currently support Safaricom airtime only. Airtel and Telkom support will be added soon.

How can I reach customer support?

For assistance, reach out to us via WhatsApp, SMS, or call at +254720293455, or email support@kredoswift.co.ke. You can also message us via our social media pages.
